Career

Working with Herald Internatonal College The professional team of Herald International College is a source of pride for the college. Our lecturers and staff members are qualified in their fields and have relevant expertise. The HIC "family" includes faculty and employees. All HIC members have a sense of belonging and a similar culture. All teachers and staff are encouraged to be involved in the lives of students as mentors and role models, in addition to their academic obligations. Collaboration is emphasized, as is a commitment to maintaining professional competency through continuing education.
